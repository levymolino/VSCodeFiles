Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Migs Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmallow (Optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave just below a boil
  3. pour milk into the mug. stirring gently as you pour
  4. stir until mix is fully dissolve in the milk
  5. top with Marshmallow if you must

Cocoa Facts

Do you know the difference?

Cocoa

Cocoa is the powdered product after processing cacao beans and has little or now cacao butter.

Chocolate

Chocolate is basically cocoa with the cacao butter and often sugar and milk
